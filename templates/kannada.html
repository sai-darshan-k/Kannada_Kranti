About Us

We are a team dedicated to advancing technology for the Kannada language. Our mission is to enhance accessibility and usability of Kannada texts through innovative solutions like OCR and handwriting recognition.

Our goal is to promote the Kannada language and culture by integrating technology into daily use cases, making it easier for people to interact with Kannada content.
